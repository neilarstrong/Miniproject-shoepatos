Nike is Working with Communities to Increase Climate Resilience.
September 19, 2022

What to Know?

  • With climate change impacting athletes around the world, Nike is working on solutions that help protect the planet and with it, the future of sport.
  • Nike is launching its new Community Climate Resilience Program with an inaugural $2M grant to Trust for Public Land.
  • The program will fund projects advancing climate resilience and access to public green spaces and sport in communities disproportionally impacted by climate threats.
  • In its initial round of funding, Nike’s work with Trust for Public Land will focus on New York City, Los Angeles and Chicago.
